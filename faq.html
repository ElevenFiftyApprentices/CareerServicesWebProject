FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

+I am interested in one of your graduates. How can I arrange an interview?

Fill out the employer form

+Are there any program fees associated with hiring an EFA graduate?

EFA does not charge any fees for placing an EFA graduate. Recruiters are available to place EFA graduates but all contracts (and associated fees) are between the recruiter and that company.
There are no fees associated with hiring an EFA graduate directly from EFA.

+How can my company qualify for the $1000?

Hire an EFA graduate for a full-time developer, project manager, or related position for a full month (40 hours per week for 30 calendar days).

+What are the different ways my company can use the $1,000?

  • EFA will issue a check to your company for $1,000.
  • You can elect to pay down the graduate’s EFA student balance.
  • You can elect to bank a credit with EFA to use later.

+In addition to their core language what other soft skills, frameworks, training, etc. have the EFA graduates acquired?

Graduates are proficient with HTML, CSS, Git, SDLC, and Agile. They have also received introductory courses in
security, deployment, UX/UI, testing, and contract law (including intellectual property).

+Are there other benefits associated with hiring an EFA graduate?

Besides getting a skilled, motivated employee Eleven Fifty offers free courses to former students for up to a year.
That means you can get additional free training in new technology to complement a new hire's pre-existing skills.

+I want to hire. What are the terms?

No special terms, just a bonus to help with your onboarding costs! If you employ the graduate full-time for 30 calendar days, you are eligible for $1,000.
Offer expires 12/31/16 and is only valid for directly placed positions.
